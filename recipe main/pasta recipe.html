White Sause Pasta

Ingredient how it's Used
Pasta I used semolina penne pasta. But feel free to use your favorite pasta of your choice, like chickpea pasta, quinoa pasta, spaghetti, linguine, macaroni, pasta shells, or any grain-free, gluten-free pasta. For a low-carb meal, you can use zoodles or spaghetti squash instead.
Vegetables I have used bell peppers, carrots, boiled sweet corn, and broccoli. You can also add mushrooms, fresh green peas, baby corn, black olives, or any other veggies of your choice. Chop them into small cubes so that they cook faster.
Garlic Freshly minced garlic adds a great flavor to the dish, so don't skip it.
Butter Use either unsalted or salted butter to prepare the roux.
Flour I used all-purpose flour (aka maida). You can substitute it with whole wheat flour.
Milk Use full-fat whole milk or half and half.
Heavy cream It is optional. It adds a richer and more creamy flavor to the dish. It also helps to thicken the sauce.
Cheese I used mozzarella cheese. You can use Parmesan cheese or any processed cheese like Amul.
Herbs & Spices Oregano, red chili flakes, freshly ground black pepper, and salt is used to flavor the white sauce.

Recipe

In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Then stir in the salt. Add the dry pasta to the water, and stir a few times to prevent the pasta from sticking together. step to add water, salt and dry pasta in the boiling water collage Cook the pasta according to the package instructions (about 10-12 minutes), occasionally stirring, until al dente or softer according to your desired texture. step to cook al dente penne pasta collage Once the pasta is cooked, reserve some starchy water for the sauce and drain the pasta in a colander over the sink. Set the cooked pasta aside. step to drain the cooked pasta in a colander collage Saute Veggies Heat butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saute until fragrant for about 30 seconds. step to sauce minced garlic in butter collage Then add mixed veggies like carrot, bell pepper, broccoli, sweet corn, and salt. Cook the veggies till they are tender but still crunchy, for around 2 minutes. Turn off the flame and keep it aside. step to saute veggies collage Make White Sauce (Béchamel Sauce) Melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat. Add all-purpose flour, and stir continuously until well combined and smooth. Cook for a minute until the raw smell of the flour goes away. Do not over-roast the flour, and ensure the color of the flour doesn’t change. step to prepare roux for white sauce collage Slowly add in milk and stir continuously with a whisk until no lumps are left. Then add the remaining milk and whisk to combine. Cook it for about 4-5 minutes stirring constantly, until the sauce boils and thickens. step to add milk and whisk collage Once the sauce is slightly thick, add the heavy cream, cheese, oregano, red chili flakes, black pepper, and salt. Continue stirring until the cheese has melted. When the sauce coats the back of the spoon, then it is done. The sauce will thicken as it cools. step to add cheese and spices and cook collage Make White Sauce Pasta Add the sauteed veggies and the cooked penne pasta to the sauce. Toss well to coat the pasta with creamy white sauce. (If needed, add 2-3 tablespoons of reserved starchy pasta water, it will help to bind and thicken the sauce.) step to add sauteed veggies and cooked pasta collage Lastly, transfer it to a serving plate and sprinkle some cheese and red chili flakes, if desired, and serve it immediately



Enjoy!
